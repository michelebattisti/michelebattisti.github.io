Michele Battisti

I am a Postdoc researcher at the Ifo Institute for Economic Research at the University of Munich. My research interests are in the fields of labour economics, economics of migration and applied econometrics. I did my bachelor degree at the University of Trento (Italy), my MSc at the University of York (UK) and I have completed my PhD at Simon Fraser University Vancouver, Canada) in 2012.
